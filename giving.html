Ways to Give

Volunteering at the Northland Animal Welfare Society (NAWS) offers a rewarding opportunity to make a tangible difference in the lives of animals in need. Whether you're passionate about animal care, fundraising, or community outreach, there's a role for everyone. Volunteers assist with daily operations, such as feeding and socializing animals, cleaning kennels, and supporting adoption events. Your contribution helps NAWS provide vital services like spaying and neutering, vaccinations, and sheltering. Join us in creating a compassionate community where every animal receives the care and love they deserve.

Amazon Smile is a simple and impactful way to support the Northland Animal Welfare Society (NAWS) while shopping on Amazon. When you shop through Amazon Smile, a portion of your eligible purchases is donated to NAWS at no extra cost to you. These donations help fund essential programs and services for animals in our care, including spaying and neutering, medical treatments, and adoption efforts. By selecting NAWS as your charity of choice on Amazon Smile, you're directly contributing to our mission of promoting animal welfare and providing a better future for pets in need. Join us in making a difference with every purchase you make.

Monetary donations to the Northland Animal Welfare Society (NAWS) play a crucial role in supporting our mission to advocate for and care for animals in need. Your contributions enable us to provide essential services such as spaying and neutering, vaccinations, medical treatments, and sheltering for animals awaiting adoption. Every donation, no matter the size, directly impacts the lives of animals by ensuring they receive the care and attention they deserve. Your support helps us continue our efforts in promoting responsible pet ownership and creating a compassionate community for animals. Consider making a donation today to help us make a lasting difference in the lives of pets in our care.
